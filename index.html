About Us

Hello! I’m Katherine, the owner and sole aesthetician at KatEyed Beauty Lounge, located in Burnaby. With over 20 years of experience in the beauty industry, I’ve honed my craft to provide expert lash extensions, brow lamination, tinting, and waxing. My mission is to enhance your natural beauty while ensuring you feel pampered and confident every time you leave my studio.

As a certified aesthetician, I take pride in offering personalized, one-on-one services that cater to your unique needs. I’m fully licensed, health-inspected, and committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and professionalism.

At KatEyed Beauty Lounge, every client receives the attention and care they deserve, in a relaxing and welcoming environment. I look forward to helping you achieve the beauty you deserve!
